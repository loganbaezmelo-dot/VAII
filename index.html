Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy for VAII (Virtual Assistant) VAII ("the App") is a personal virtual assistant application designed to provide search integrations and productivity tools. This policy explains how data is handled within the App.

1. Data Handling
VAII operates as a client-side application. Processing occurs directly within your browser session.
• Authentication: Credentials and login profiles are handled securely via Firebase Authentication to manage access.
• Google User Profiles: If you choose to link your Google login, the App processes read-only views of your file storage and email titles via structural calls. These access variables reside entirely in your hardware memory.
• Queries and Maps: Search parameters for weather, time, or map tracking are processed via public informational endpoints and mapping tools to render your local visual cards.

2. Integrated Services
The App communicates with standard informational tools to fulfill commands: Firebase, Google Cloud Platform (Maps, YouTube, Gmail, Drive), Open-Meteo, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, MyMemory, and Pollinations.ai.

3. Storage Security
Your session settings remain stored safely inside local browser memory. Signing out removes these variables permanently from your hardware. No continuous tracking systems run on background database servers.

4. Policy Updates
This policy may be adjusted to align with future assistant updates.
